Question

Project Euler question 5, Smallest Multiple:

2520 is the smallest number that can be divided by each of the numbers from 1 to 10 without any remainder.

What is the smallest positive number that is evenly divisible by all of the numbers from 1 to 20?

Answer

The smallest number that is evenly divisible by every number from 1 to 20 is the number which has all of their prime factors, without any duplicates. For example, since 16 = 2^4, the answer must contain the prime factors 2^4; and while 8 has three factors of 2, those would duplicate the factors of 16 and so do not need to be included.
